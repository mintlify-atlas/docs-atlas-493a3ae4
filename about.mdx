## What is Chokmah?

Chokmah is a personal knowledge repository designed to serve as a second brain - a carefully curated space for storing and organizing key elements from various subjects that deserve easy access and quick reference.

<Info>
The project functions as a digital garden where knowledge is cultivated, organized, and made readily accessible when needed.
</Info>

## The Meaning Behind the Name

**Chokmah** (חָכְמָה) is the Hebrew word for "wisdom". In the mystical tradition of Kabbalah, it represents the second sephirah in the Tree of Life, positioned at the top of the pillar of force/masculine energy.

<Note>
Chokmah symbolizes the primordial celestial intellect - the initial point of divine wisdom that emanates from God's will before being shaped by understanding (Binah). It represents pure, undifferentiated wisdom at its source.
</Note>

The name reflects the project's essence: a wellspring of wisdom, carefully organized and always accessible.

## Purpose & Philosophy

<Tip>
"Chokmah was created with the objective of serving as a second brain; storing some elements of each subject that I would like to have easy access to."
</Tip>

This documentation represents more than just a collection of notes - it's a living record of a learning journey. It captures:

- **Learnings**: Key concepts and insights gained through study and practice
- **Ideas**: Thoughts and concepts that emerge along the way
- **References**: Quick access to important information that might otherwise be forgotten
- **Growth**: A chronicle of technical and intellectual development

The philosophy is simple: in a world overflowing with information, Chokmah provides a curated, personal space where the most valuable knowledge is preserved and easily retrievable.
